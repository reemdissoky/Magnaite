01 · CCTV & AI Analytics

03 · Networking & Connectivity

The infrastructure that holds everything else up.

The problem: when the network drops, every other system goes with it. POS down. Cameras blind. Signage frozen. Online orders bouncing. Most multi-branch operators have networks pieced together across years and vendors — different gear at every site, different failure modes at every site.

What we install: structured cabling, managed switches, dual-WAN routing, and enterprise Wi-Fi — engineered to behave the same way at every location. Issues diagnosed remotely. Traffic re-routed automatically when one upstream fails. The branch keeps trading even when one path goes down.

Sized to your phase. We don't oversize the deployment for an operation you don't yet have. The infrastructure grows when you grow.

05 · Transaction & POS

Payment infrastructure that holds the rush.

The problem: when POS goes down during peak hours, the line stops, the orders bounce, and the shift loses revenue that doesn't come back. Most POS infrastructure isn't engineered for the actual velocity of QSR or coffee operations during the Friday rush.

What we install: payment and ordering infrastructure designed around the realities of high-volume Saudi F&B — multi-shift teams, kitchen display integration, back-office reporting, and tested integrations with the POS platforms operators already run. Built so the rush is the test it passes, not the moment it fails.

Cost-effective at every scale. Whether you're at three locations or three hundred, the deployment is sized to the actual throughput — not over-engineered to a spec sheet.
