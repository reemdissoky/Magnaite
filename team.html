Careers

Want to join Magnaite?

We hire for fluency in what's current — not loyalty to what used to work. The team renews continuously because the systems we install keep evolving, and the people deploying them have to stay ahead of that curve.

Open across engineering, sales, operations, marketing, and field deployment. If you've got the curiosity to keep up with how kingdom infrastructure actually works — and the discipline to deliver against a ten-year standard — we'd like to hear from you.

What we look for:

Engineering and technical roles · Field install and deployment · Sales and account management · Marketing and content · Operations and procurement · Customer support
