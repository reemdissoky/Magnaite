Current partners

The platforms we deploy.

Authorized partnerships across networking, video, AI loss prevention, digital signage, POS, and cybersecurity. Plus government registrations and industry standards we operate against.

National Cybersecurity Authority

Magnaite is a registered partner with Saudi Arabia's National Cybersecurity Authority — the kingdom's regulatory body for cybersecurity standards and infrastructure protection. Required for the cybersecurity work we do across enterprise and government clients.

Ruijie

Ruijie Networks

New partnership signed in 2026 for enterprise networking infrastructure. Brings managed switches, infrastructure-grade reliability, and centralized network management to the Magnaite stack — extending what we can offer multi-branch operators across the kingdom.

Authorized partner for cloud-based video management. Deployed across our F&B and retail networks for centralized monitoring, secure storage, and remote access from any operations dashboard.
