The problem

When POS goes down, the shift loses revenue that doesn't come back.

The Friday rush is the moment your POS infrastructure earns its budget. When the line stops because terminals are unresponsive or transactions aren’t completing, the orders bounce, the customers leave, and the revenue is gone. Most POS infrastructure isn’t engineered for the actual velocity Saudi F&B operations hit during peak.

Transaction infrastructure built for the rush.
