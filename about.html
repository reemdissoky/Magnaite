About Magnaite

Most firms
fade. We didn't.

Founded in 1989 by Saudi engineers who wanted to build the country's quiet infrastructure. Thirty-five years later, the same firm — different generation at the helm, same instinct: stay through every iteration. The kingdom changed shape five times. We're still here.

The story

Thirty-five years of staying current.

Magnaite was founded in 1989 in Riyadh. We started with the layer underneath — wiring, cabling, the infrastructure no one notices until it stops working. Then phones. Then networks. Then cameras. Then control rooms. Now AI, cloud architecture, cybersecurity at kingdom scale.

What's stayed constant is the standard. What's evolved is everything else — the technologies we deploy, the engineering practices we follow, the talent on the team. Magnaite renews continuously. We hire for fluency in what's current, not loyalty to what used to work. The team running the firm today operates at the speed of the latest infrastructure and the most current compliance frameworks. That's how a Saudi firm stays relevant for thirty-five years.

Heritage gives us perspective on what holds up over a decade. The team's youth gives us pace to deploy what works today. Both, in the same firm, in the same project — that's the rare combination. It's why clients across food and beverage, government, retail, and enterprise have stayed with us. Most for over a decade. Some longer.

What we believe

Six principles
we don't bend.

01

Install once, stay through every iteration.

The work doesn't end at handover. The relationship does. Most firms call that done. We call it the start.

02

Substance over surface.

The thing that matters is what runs at 2am on a Friday, not the marketing deck on Monday morning.

03

Tenure is the only honest metric.

Anyone can win a deal. The proof is whether they're still on the call ten years later when something needs adjustment.

04

Heritage and instinct, in the same room.

The team running Magnaite today is in their thirties and forties. Old enough to have seen the kingdom change. Young enough to build for what comes next.

05

Saudi-rooted, kingdom-scale.

Every client, every install, every team member operates inside the kingdom's reality — not from a slide imported from elsewhere.

06

Built around your phase.

Every Magnaite engagement is sized to where you actually are — three branches or three hundred, just opening or already scaling. We don't oversell. We don't lock you into infrastructure that outsizes the operation. The deployment grows when the business grows. Cost stays in proportion to value delivered.

Vision 2030

The kingdom is rebuilding. We've been part of it the whole time.

Vision 2030 isn't an external program for Magnaite — it's the operating context. Every system we deploy carries digital transformation, NCA cybersecurity compliance, and Saudi-rooted operations into the next chapter.

We work with clients across F&B, government, and retail to build infrastructure that meets the kingdom's transformation goals: localized operations, integrated technology, and resilient compliance.
